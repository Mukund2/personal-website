Dual-Use Tech Bridge

Stack Overflow for hardware.

A large corpus of U.S. military technology is already public—scattered across SBIR/STTR grants, DTIC publications, DoD patents, and DARPA-funded research—but written in jargon that never maps to commercial applications. Engineers at drone startups keep reinventing things DARPA funded three years ago. They just don't know it exists.

Dual-Use Tech Bridge is an AI-powered platform that maps public U.S. military technology to commercial applications. I ingested ~120,000 technologies from federal open-data sources into a searchable web app, with an inference layer that explains, in plain English, how a given military technology could improve a commercial product—and vice versa.
